# Story 4.1: Footer & Global Links

## Status
Done

## Story
**As a** Visitor,
**I want** to easily access legal information, social links, and quick navigation at the bottom of the page,
**so that** I can stay connected with Yash Labs.

## Acceptance Criteria
1.  **Layout:** Multi-column footer container (Logo/Bio, Quick Links, Legal, Socials).
2.  **Visual Style:** Glassmorphism background (`bg-black/20` or similar dark overlay) with a top border (`border-white/10`).
3.  **Content:**
    *   **Brand:** Logo + tagline.
    *   **Navigation:** Links to Home, Services, About, Contact.
    *   **Legal:** Privacy Policy, Terms of Service (links can be placeholders).
    *   **Socials:** Icons for Twitter, LinkedIn, GitHub (Lucide React icons).
4.  **Copyright:** "© 2024 Yash Labs. All rights reserved."
5.  **Hover Effects:** Links should change color (e.g., to Cyan-500) on hover.
6.  **Responsiveness:** Stacked columns on mobile, 4-column grid on desktop.
7.  **Semantic HTML:** Use `<footer>` tag.
